Fellows are offered a unique opportunity to unlock their entrepreneurial mindset and gain hands-on experiential learning by working at an early-stage venture with a fast-paced and high growth environment. Fellows are provided the path to accelerate their careers as we help align their academic goals with their professional aspirations - curated curriculum delivered by industry professionals focused on meeting employer needs through in-demand skills for tech careers
What we Offer
VFP THINKTANK
Introductions to multidisciplinary roles such as Product Management, User Experience Design, Data Science, Business Development with masterclasses from young industry professionals in the industry and keynotes from industry executives.
VFP STARTUP SUCCESS BOOTCAMP
Demystifying startups, its fundamentals, tools for success and guiding principles to solve critical business challenges
VFP CAREER ACCELERATOR
One-on-One personal coaching and mentorship from professionals at top tech companies such as Google, Facebook, Uber, Amazon, Square, Doordash etc.

About Us
Venture For Pakistan is a Summer Fellowship for University Students who want to become the founders of tomorrow and help build Pakistan’s tech ecosystem from the ground up. Venture for Pakistan recruits top undergraduate students, puts them through rigorous training supplemented with a robust industry-focused curriculum and matches them with hands-on internship opportunities at startups. ‍

Fellows will then receive 1:1 coaching and mentorship from industry professionals at top technology companies such as Google, Facebook, Amazon, Uber etc. This experience consists of direct coaching of how to break into tech, its various career paths and the art of packaging work experiences on to resumes and in interviews.
Our Partners
We hunt for Pakistan's most promising startups with great leaders and teams who will give our Felllows the experience of a liftime. The partners help us cultivate early career talent to help build Pakistan's tech ecosystem from the ground up.
